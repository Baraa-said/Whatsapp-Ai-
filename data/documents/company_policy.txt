# TechCorp Company Policies

## Working Hours
- Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday
- Flexible working arrangements are available upon manager approval
- Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week

## Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
- All employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- Unused leave can be carried over (maximum 5 days)

### Sick Leave
- Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days

### Parental Leave
- Maternity leave: 16 weeks paid leave
- Paternity leave: 4 weeks paid leave
- Adoption leave: 12 weeks paid leave

## Expense Reimbursement
- Business travel expenses are fully reimbursed
- Receipts must be submitted within 30 days
- Meals during travel: up to $50 per day
- Hotel accommodations: up to $200 per night

## IT Security Guidelines
1. Passwords must be at least 12 characters long
2. Two-factor authentication is mandatory
3. Company devices must not be used for personal activities
4. Report any suspicious emails to security@techcorp.com

## Code of Conduct
- Treat all colleagues with respect
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- No discrimination or harassment of any kind
- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed immediately

## Contact Information
- HR Department: hr@techcorp.com
- IT Support: support@techcorp.com
- Emergency: +1-800-TECHCORP
